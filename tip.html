Back by popular demand! Sequins & Scrums, the drag show fundraiser hosted by Washington Scandals Rugby is back on Friday, February 23rd at Town Danceboutique. The money raised will be used to help send the Scandals to Bingham Cup, the world cup of gay rugby. Bingham Cup is named for Mark Bingham, a gay rugby pioneer who died on 9/11 aboard Flight 93. Are you interested in trying rugby? The Scandals are hosting a rugby 101 the day following the show. RSVP here for details.

From now until February 22, we are accepting donations in the form of "tips" for the queens. Browse through our ten queens below, and tip your favorites! Tips are being collected via Venmo. (No Venmo account? click here) All money collected goes to the Scandals Bingham Cup fund, but the queen who receives the most tips will automatically advance to the final, where she will lip sync for her life in the hopes of becoming Scrum Queen 2018. Totals are updated every few days. Tips sent on or after show day will still go to our Bingham fund but may not be counted towards your queen.

For all the latest info about Sequins and Scrums 2018, RSVP to our Facebook event!
